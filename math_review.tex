\subsubsection{Radius of Curvature}

The radius of curvature, $R$, of a curve at a point is a measure of the radius of the circular arc which best approximates the curve at that point.

For $a_t = 0$

\[
R= \abs{\frac{v^2}{a_c}}
\]
